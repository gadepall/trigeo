
\item A bag contains $5$ red balls and some blue balls. If the probability of drawing a blue ball from the bag is three times that of a red ball, find the number of blue balls in the bag.
\item A card  is drawn at random from a well shuffled pack of $52$ playing  cards. Find the probability of getting
\begin{enumerate}[label = (\roman*)]
\item a red king  \item a queen or a jack.
\end{enumerate}
\end{enumerate}

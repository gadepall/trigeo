\solution 
From 
	\eqref{eq:median-be}
	and
	\eqref{eq:median-cf},
the equations of $BE$ 
and 
$CF$
are, respectively,
\begin{align}
\myvec{3 & 1} \vec{x} &= \myvec{-6}
\label{eq:1.2.3,8}
\\
\myvec{ 5&-1} \vec{x} &= \myvec{-10}
\label{eq:1.2.3,9}
\end{align}
From \eqref{eq:1.2.3,8} and \eqref{eq:1.2.3,9} the augmented matrix is
\begin{align}
    \label{eq:matrowoperations}
    \myvec{
    3 & 1 & -6
    \\
    5 & -1 & -10
    }
     \xleftrightarrow[]{R_1 \leftarrow R_1+R_2}
    \myvec{
    8 & 0 & -16
    \\
    5 & -1 & -10 
    }
    \\
     \xleftrightarrow[]{R_1\leftarrow R_1/8}
    \myvec{
    1 & 0 & -2
    \\
    5 & -1 & -10 
    }
     \xleftrightarrow[]{R_2\leftarrow R_2-5R_1}
    \myvec{
    1 & 0 & -2
    \\
    0 & -1 & 0
    }
    \\
     \xleftrightarrow[]{R_2\leftarrow -R_2}
    \myvec{
    1 & 0 & -2
    \\
    0 & 1 & 0
    }
\end{align} 
using Gauss elimination.  Therefore, 
\begin{align}
\vec{G} = \myvec{-2 \\ 0}
	\label{eq:median-g}
\end{align}
